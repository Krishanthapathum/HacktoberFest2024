About HacktoberFest 2024

HacktoberFest is a month-long celebration of open source software, encouraging contributions to various projects. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned developer, there's something for everyone!

Join us in making the open source community better by contributing your code, participating in discussions, or even just learning from others!

How to Contribute

Follow these steps to get involved:

  1. Sign up on the official HacktoberFest website.
  2. Find a project you'd like to contribute to on GitHub.
  3. Make your contributions (pull requests) to the project.
  4. Share your contributions on social media using #HacktoberFest.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is HacktoberFest? HacktoberFest is an annual event where developers are encouraged to contribute to open source projects.

Who can participate? Anyone can participate, regardless of skill level!

Is there a fee to join? No, HacktoberFest is free to join.
